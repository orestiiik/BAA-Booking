Create - build your own prototype

\

CREATE is a students workshop or hub. It supports development and creation of prototypes ranging from software to electronics to creating physical mockups and 3D printed designs.
The students that are maintaining CREATE, are there to help you in creating prototypes or to help you get in the right direction to make it yourself.



Our CREATE lab allows you to try out:


  • 3D printing
  • Virtual Reality
  • Foil cutting
  • Vaccuum Forming
  • Creating smaller prototypes consisting of electronics, hardware and software
