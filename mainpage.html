How our system works?

Use your student card to borrow books at the library.Your username is your eaa-username (ex. eaasist) and your pin codeis the last four digits of your CPR number.It is free to use the library and it is open until 6PM for all students with a student ID. Use the self-service machine by the library entrance to borrow and return books.
